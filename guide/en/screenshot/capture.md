# Capture

## Start capturing

In the **Debug** tab, click **Screenshot**, or press `Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+F`.

> If the shortcut doesn't respond, it may be clashing with your OS or another extension — feel free to start from the button instead.

## Drag a region

Drag to select the region you want to capture. You can crop just the part where the bug shows up instead of the whole page, so the reader knows exactly where to look.

![Dragging a region](../assets/dummy.jpg)

## Output

The selected region is captured as an image exactly as it currently appears (viewport-based). Once capture is done, you move on to annotation naturally.

> See [Annotation](annotation.md) for how to draw on it.
